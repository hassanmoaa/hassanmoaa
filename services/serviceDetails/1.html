Green Transformation: Global Trend, Benefits to people, Society and Environment

It’s known that climate footprint of healthcare facilities presents approximately 5% of net global emissions, and that chemical pollution and the irrational use of resources in the healthcare sector exacerbate health problems in the world and negatively affect public health. Accordingly, there has been a growing appetite for sustainability, and green transition has become rewarding and of great importance, being one of the urban transformation processes expressed as a system which puts emphasis on the use of renewable energy sources and green areas for the sustainable future of cities aiming to minimize the negative effects on the environment and human activities
